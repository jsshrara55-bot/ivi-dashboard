## Slide 2: The Challenge Insurance Companies Face Today

Insurance companies struggle with fragmented data across multiple systems, making it nearly impossible to get a unified view of client health. Key pain points include:

- No predictive capabilities to identify at-risk clients early

## Slide 3: Introducing the Intelligent Value Index (IVI)

The IVI Dashboard solves this by creating a single, actionable metric that combines three critical pillars of client value:

**Health Pillar (H):** Analyzes claims patterns, approval rates, and medical utilization to understand the financial health of each corporate account.

**Experience Pillar (E):** Measures customer satisfaction through call center interactions, response times, and issue resolution rates.

**Utilization Pillar (U):** Tracks service efficiency including pre-authorization usage, provider network engagement, and benefit optimization.

The weighted IVI formula produces a score from 0-100, enabling instant comparison and prioritization across your entire portfolio.
