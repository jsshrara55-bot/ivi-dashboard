<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Neumorphism (Soft UI)
  **Core Principles**:
  - Soft shadows and light gradients to create depth
  - Minimalist color palette with subtle contrasts
  - Rounded corners and fluid shapes
  - Focus on tactile interaction and realism
  **Color Philosophy**:
  - Background: Off-white (#F0F0F3) or soft gray
  - Accents: Soft blue (#4A90E2) for primary actions
  - Text: Dark gray (#333333) for readability
  - Intent: Create a calming, professional environment that feels modern and approachable.
  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - Card-based layout with floating elements
  - Asymmetric grid for visual interest
  - Sidebar navigation with soft hover effects
  **Signature Elements**:
  - Embossed/Debossed buttons and cards
  - Soft, blurred shadows (neumorphic effect)
  - Circular progress indicators with soft glows
  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - Elements should feel like they are being pressed or lifted
  - Smooth transitions and subtle scale effects on hover
  **Animation**:
  - Gentle fade-ins and slide-ups
  - Soft pulsing for alerts or notifications
  **Typography System**:
  - Headings: Nunito (Rounded, friendly)
  - Body: Roboto (Clean, legible)
</idea>

  **Design Movement**: Glassmorphism
  **Core Principles**:
  - Translucency and background blur (frosted glass effect)
  - Vivid, gradient backgrounds
  - White borders and light shadows for separation
  - Layered depth and hierarchy
  **Color Philosophy**:
  - Background: Deep, rich gradients (Purple/Blue/Pink)
  - Cards: Semi-transparent white with blur
  - Text: White or light gray for contrast
  - Intent: Evoke a sense of futuristic technology and innovation.
  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - Centralized dashboard with floating glass panels
  - Background elements visible through UI components
  - Minimalist navigation bar with glass effect
  **Signature Elements**:
  - Frosted glass cards with white borders
  - Vibrant, abstract background blobs
  - Glowing indicators and charts
  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - Elements should feel lightweight and ethereal
  - Hover effects increase opacity or glow
  **Animation**:
  - Parallax effects on background elements
  - Smooth, glass-like transitions
  **Typography System**:
  - Headings: Montserrat (Modern, geometric)
  - Body: Open Sans (Neutral, readable)
</idea>

  **Design Movement**: Swiss Style (International Typographic Style)

  - Cleanliness, readability, and objectivity
  - Asymmetric layouts and grid systems
  - Sans-serif typography as a primary design element
  - High contrast and bold use of color
  **Color Philosophy**:
  - Background: White or very light gray
  - Accents: Bold Red (#FF3B30), Blue (#007AFF), Yellow (#FFCC00)
  - Text: Black (#000000) for maximum contrast
  - Intent: Communicate data clearly and efficiently without distraction.
  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - Strict grid system with ample whitespace
  - Asymmetric balance and dynamic composition
  - Modular components with clear hierarchy
  **Signature Elements**:
  - Bold, large typography for headings and metrics
  - Geometric shapes and lines for separation
  - Minimalist icons and charts
  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - Direct and responsive feedback
  - Sharp, precise transitions
  **Animation**:
  - Snappy, linear animations
  - Elements slide into place with precision
  **Typography System**:
  - Headings: Helvetica Now (Classic, bold)
  - Body: Inter (Modern, versatile)
