# IVI Dashboard - Evaluation Criteria Analysis

## Vision 2030 Alignment

The IVI Dashboard is designed to align with Saudi Vision 2030's focus on:
- **Preventive Healthcare**: Health Score (H) tracks chronic disease management and preventable claims
- **Digital Transformation**: Full web-based dashboard with real-time analytics
- **Customer Experience**: Experience Score (E) measures service quality and satisfaction

## Three Pillars Coverage

### 1. Health Outcomes (H Score)
- **Dashboard Location**: Component Scores chart, Client Details page
- **Features**:
  - Claims analysis by type (inpatient, outpatient, pharmacy)
  - Claims ratio tracking
  - Chronic condition indicators
  - Preventable claims identification

### 2. Experience Quality (E Score)
- **Dashboard Location**: Component Scores chart, Call Center metrics
- **Features**:
  - Customer satisfaction scores (1-5)
  - Call resolution rates
  - Complaint handling tracking
  - Response time metrics

### 3. Cost Sustainability (U Score)
- **Dashboard Location**: Component Scores chart, Utilization metrics
- **Features**:
  - Network usage percentage
  - Pre-authorization compliance
  - Cost per member tracking
  - Claims pattern analysis
